Circuit Theory and Soviet Union
Year - 1962

Circuit Switching is a technology used where the circuit is first established between two point and then a message could be sent between those two points. However the US government realized that if the Soviet union would ever attack them it could completely bring down the telecommunication infrastructure at that time because the connection between two points would not be established. Thus the government pushed ARPA to come up with a solution to this problem, thus accelerating computer research.
